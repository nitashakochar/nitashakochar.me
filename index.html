I’ve only been at DALI for a term so far, but intend to work in both design and development. At the DALI lab, not only am I able to improve upon my problem-solving skills by working with different points of view, but I also have constant exposure to creativity and innovation—two things that I truly appreciate. At Dartmouth, I plan to pursue a major in Anthropology with a minor in computer science and a minor in human-centered design.
