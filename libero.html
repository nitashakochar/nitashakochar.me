UX/UI Design

Client

Dartmouth Student through the Dartmouth Applied Learning and Innovation (DALI) Lab.

Goal

Create a mobile app that would encourage people to get fit by virtually working-out with a person with a similar fitness level (anywhere in the world) in real-time.

Background

Exercise is challenging for people who are not easily motivated, and gyms and personal training costs tend to run high. Research has shown that competition is an influential motivator. Using an app that can connect you with someone at your fitness-level will provide you with an outside source of encouragement to help you engage in some healthy competition.
