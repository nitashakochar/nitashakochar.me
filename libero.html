Background:

Exercise is challenging for people who are not easily motivated, and gyms and personal training costs tend to run high. Research has shown that competition is an influential motivator. Using an app that can connect you with someone at your fitness-level can provide you with an outside source of encouragement that can help you engage in some healthy competition. Essentially, this is an app that aims to encourage people to get fit by virtually working-out with a person who has a similar fitness level (anywhere in the world) in real-time.

Client:

Dartmouth Student through the Dartmouth Applied Learning and Innovation (DALI) Lab.

Goal:

Create an MVP mobile app within 9 weeks.
